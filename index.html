The Top 10 Most Popular Cocktails

PORNSTAR MARTINI

  • 1 1/2 ounces vanilla flavored vodka
  • 1/2 ounce passion fruit liqueur
  • 1 ounce passion fruit puree
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce vanilla simple syrup
  • 2 ounces sparkling wine, chilled

BLUE HAWAIIAN

  • 3/4 ounce vodka
  • 3/4 ounce light rum
  • 1/2 ounce blue curaçao
  • 3 ounces pineapple juice
  • 1 ounce sweet and sour mix

BARBADOS SURPRISE

  • 1/2 ounce Grenadine
  • 2 ounces Orange Juice
  • 2 ounces Light Rum
  • 1/2 ounce Blue Curacao

POMEGRANATE GIN SLING

  • 1 1/2 ounces gin
  • 1 1/2 ounces pomegranate juice
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • Club soda, optional

COSMOPOLITAN

  • 1 1/2 ounces citron vodka
  • 3/4 ounce Cointreau
  • 3/4 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce cranberry juice cocktail

SINGAPORE SLING

  • 3/4 ounce gin
  • 1/4 ounce Benedictine
  • 1/4 ounce Grand Marnier
  • 1/4 ounce Heering cherry liqueur
  • 1 ounce pineapple juice
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • Club soda, chilled, to top

BLUE MARGARITA

  • Coarse salt, for optional rim
  • 1 1/2 ounces tequila
  • 1 ounce blue curaçao liqueur
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice

MAI TAI

  • 1 1/2 ounces white rum
  • 3/4 ounce orange curaçao
  • 3/4 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce orgeat
  • 1/2 ounce dark rum

KISS ON THE LIPS

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 1/2 ounces Malibu Rum
  • 1 1/2 ounces peach schnapps
  • 1 tablespoon grenadine, or to taste

WICKED WITCH COCKTAIL

  • 1 1/2 ounces Whiskey
  • 1/4 ounce Maraschino Liqueur
  • 1/4 ounce Pineapple Juice
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
